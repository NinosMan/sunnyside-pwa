Hey, we're SunnySide

Your personal beach scout — powered by wind science.

🌊 What we do

SunnySide helps you find the most sheltered, comfortable beach near you — right now, based on live wind and terrain data.

We scan dozens of beaches in your area, check the current wind conditions at each one, model how nearby hills and headlands affect shelter, and give every beach a simple comfort score so you can just show up and relax.

🏅 How we score beaches

Each beach gets a comfort score from 0–100 based on a few key factors:

  • Wind speed & gusts — lower is better. We model wind at human height (~2m), not the 10m weather station standard.
  • Terrain shelter — hills and headlands upwind reduce felt wind significantly. We sample elevation in a cone upwind to estimate this.
  • Shore orientation — a beach facing directly into the wind feels harsher than one running parallel to it.
  • Sun & cloud cover — a sunny day bumps the score; heavy overcast knocks it down a little.
  • Distance — a slight penalty for beaches farther away, so a mediocre spot nearby can still beat a great one an hour away.

📡 Where the data comes from

  • Beach locations — OpenStreetMap via the Overpass API. We search for beaches, bays, coves and beach resorts tagged in the map.
  • Live wind — Open-Meteo, a free open-source weather API. Wind speed, gusts, and direction updated hourly.
  • Elevation / terrain — Also Open-Meteo's elevation endpoint, based on SRTM 90m DEM data.
  • Geocoding — Photon by Komoot, powered by OpenStreetMap data.
  • Maps — Leaflet.js with Esri World Topo basemap tiles.

All data sources are free and open. SunnySide does not store your location on any server — everything runs in your browser.
